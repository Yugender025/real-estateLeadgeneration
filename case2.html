Project Overview


Project Name: Anvita High9


Project Location: Prime residential area in Bangalore, known for its connectivity but faced challenges due to nearby construction sites affecting the ambiance.

Challenges

Despite the premium offering, Anvita high9 struggled to reach its target audience effectively due to high competition and fragmented marketing efforts. Additionally, location-related drawbacks such as noise pollution and temporary roadblocks influenced customer perception.

Solutions

We implemented a comprehensive digital marketing strategy focusing on hyper-local targeting to emphasize connectivity benefits while downplaying location drawbacks. Engaging creative campaigns were crafted, including virtual tours, influencer collaborations, and community engagement videos, to create a positive narrative around the location.

Gender Distribution Analysis

  • Male Demographic (80%): Primarily engaged through investment-focused content, showing higher interest in financial aspects and property appreciation potential.
  • Female Demographic (20%): Demonstrated stronger engagement with lifestyle features, community aspects, and security amenities.
  • Targeting Strategy: Customized content delivery based on gender preferences resulted in 35% higher engagement rates.
  • Joint Decision Making: Campaigns targeting both demographics showed 45% higher conversion rates.

Marketing Budget Allocation Impact

  • Meta Ads (65%): Generated 3,000 leads with a cost per lead of ₹200, primarily targeting families and mature audiences.
  • Google Ads (25%): Captured 2,500 high-intent leads through search campaigns, focusing on property seekers in specific locations.
  • Others (10%): Complementary channels including email marketing and local events, contributing 500 leads.

Lead Generation Funnel Performance

  • Total Leads (6,000): Achieved through multi-channel marketing with a 15% higher reach than industry average.
  • Pre-qualified Leads (4,800): 26.7% qualification rate, significantly above the market average of 20%.
  • Site Visits (1,150): 25% conversion from qualified leads, optimized through personalized follow-ups.

Conclusion

Through strategic digital marketing and efficient lead management, Anvita high9 achieved exceptional results in lead generation and sales conversion. This case study showcases the power of a well-planned, multi-channel digital marketing approach in the real estate sector.
